About

We are a group of coders who are extremely passionate about saving the environment. We want to promote awareness for reducing food waste through an easy to use, intuitive app. This app will make your life easier and reduce food waste at the same time.

The WhatsInMyFridge app will make your shopping lists easier to make, and keeps track of your groceries after they are bought. The app makes it effortless to find out how to store your foods and when they will expire.
